## nepali-calendar-engine

---

### Project

A comprehensive Bikram Sambat calendar engine providing:
- BS ↔ AD date conversion
- Calendar grid generation
- Panchang data (tithi, paksha, nakshatra)
- 40+ festival definitions with tithi-based & fixed-date resolution
- Auspicious/inauspicious date classification
- Bilingual support (English & Nepali)

### Data Sources

| Source | Used For |
|---|---|
| Nepal Rashtriya Panchang | Primary tithi & nakshatra reference |
| Drik Panchang | Validation & cross-reference |
| Nepal Government Publications | Public holidays & national events |
